Simien Mountains Trekking Adventure

Simien Mountains National Park 5 Days 4.9 (24 reviews)

Tour Description

This 5-day trek through the Simien Mountains National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offers breathtaking views of jagged mountain peaks, deep valleys, and sheer cliffs that drop some 1,500 meters. You'll encounter unique wildlife including the Gelada baboon, the Walia ibex, and the Ethiopian wolf.

The trek is moderately challenging, with daily hikes of 4-6 hours at altitudes between 3,000 and 4,000 meters. Our expert local guides will ensure your safety while sharing their deep knowledge of the region's ecology and culture.

Highlights

  • Trek to Ras Dashen, Ethiopia's highest peak at 4,550m
  • Visit traditional Ethiopian villages
  • Spot endemic wildlife including Gelada baboons
  • Camp under the stars in pristine mountain environments
  • Experience Ethiopian hospitality and cuisine
